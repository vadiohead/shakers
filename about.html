About Us

Shakes that hit.
Burgers that stack.

We are a family-owned burger joint located in the heart of the Seattle's University District. We are passionate about creating delicious, high-quality burgers and shakes that satisfy our customers' cravings. Our menu features a variety of mouth-watering options, including classic cheeseburgers, loaded fries, and creamy milkshakes.

We use only the freshest ingredients and cook everything to order, ensuring that every bite is bursting with flavor. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a late-night snack, we've got you covered with our tasty offerings and friendly service.
